Instructions & Rules for the Quiz

  • Each team will be asked questions in turn.
  • Three question types: MCQ, True/False, and Fill in the Blank.
  • A correct answer on your own turn earns 4 marks.
  • If answered incorrectly, the question passes to the next team:
    • 1st Pass → 3 marks  |  2nd Pass → 2 marks  |  3rd Pass → 1 mark
    • After 3 passes, the question ends with no marks.
  • The timer turns red below 10 s; at 0, the screen flashes red.
  • For Fill in the Blank: type your answer and press Submit Answer or Enter.
  • Click Show Correct Answer then Next Question to continue.
  • Progress is auto-saved after every question. Use 💾 Save Now or 📤 Export for extra safety.
  • Be honest — no discussing answers with other teams.
